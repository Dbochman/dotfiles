========================================
Key Takeaways from This Example:
========================================

1. Bulk Operations: Identified newsletter pattern and archived 4 emails at once
2. Flagging Important Items: Used flags to mark 4 action items for follow-up
3. Quick Decisions: Archive/Read/Flag without reading every email in detail
4. Time Efficiency: Processed 10 emails in ~5 minutes
5. Remaining Work: Clear view of what's left (13 emails) and what needs action (4 flags)

Typical triage session processes 10-20 emails, identifies patterns for bulk
operations, and flags important items. Sessions typically take 5-15 minutes
depending on inbox size and email complexity.
